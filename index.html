About Us

TomSpeed ​​is one of the newest and most important brands of Office Express DOOEL - Gostivar. The project started in 2010. with the signing of the agreement and the beginning of the cooperation between Volkswagen Financial Service and Office Express DOOEL, which cooperation as an idea was born thanks to the vision of both companies, the experience and the love for motoring.

The success of the whole project is due to previous research in the used car market and the desire to offer something more to customers. To offer quality, to offer security, to offer availability and all that at a decent price. As a guarantee for the above, of course, is the world's largest car group Volkswagen and the two best-selling car brands AUDI and VOLKSWAGEN, which are slowly but surely fully conquering both the world and the Macedonian car market.

In recent years, AutoWelt has completely won over home buyers and established itself as a leader in the sale of used cars in our market. Successful and satisfied customers are our main motto that we have managed to impose in the past years and to raise the criteria for the sale of used cars.
